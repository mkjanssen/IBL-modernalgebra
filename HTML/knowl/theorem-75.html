Theorem 4.3.5.

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring with identity \(1_R\) and \(I\) an ideal of \(R\text{.}\) The set of equivalence classes modulo \(I\text{,}\) denoted \(R/I\text{,}\) is a commutative ring with identity \(1_R + I\) under the operations of addition modulo \(I\) and multiplication modulo \(I\) defined in Theorem 4.3.3 and Theorem 4.3.4.
