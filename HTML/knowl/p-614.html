It is a straightforward definition check to see that \(I\) as defined in the hint is an ideal. That we are in a PID means there exists \(a\) such that \(I=\cup I_j=\ideal{a}\text{.}\) Therefore there exists \(j\) such that \(a\in I_j\text{.}\) It follows that \(I=I_j\) and in particular that \(I=I_k\) for all \(k\geq j\text{.}\)
