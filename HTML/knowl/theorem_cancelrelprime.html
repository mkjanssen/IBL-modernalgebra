Theorem 1.4.16.

Let \(a,b,c\text{,}\) and \(m\) be integers with \(m > 1\) and \(\gcd(c,m)=1\text{.}\) Then there is some \(x\in \Z\) such that \(\overline{c} \overline{x} = \overline{1}\text{.}\)

Conclude that if \(\overline{a} \cdot\overline{c} = \overline{b}\cdot\overline{c}\) in \(\Z_m\) that \(\overline{a} = \overline{b}\text{.}\)

Proof.
