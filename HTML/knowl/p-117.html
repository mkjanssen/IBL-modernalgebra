Now, assume by way of contradiction, that \(r_i \ne t_i\) for some \(i\text{.}\) Without loss of generality, we may assume \(r_i \lt t_i\text{.}\) Then \(p_i^{t_i} | p_1^{r_1} p_2^{r_2} \cdots p_m^{r_m}\text{,}\) which implies that

\begin{equation*} p_i^{t_i - r_i} | p_1^{r_1} p_2^{r_2} \cdots \hat{p}_i^{r_i} \cdots p_m^{r_m}\text{.} \end{equation*}
