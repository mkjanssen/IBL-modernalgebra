The units are \(R^\times\text{.}\) Clearly, \(R^\times\subseteq R[x]^\times\text{.}\)

Suppose \(p(x),q(x)\in R[x]^\times\) such that \(p(x) q(x) = 1\text{.}\) Then \(\deg p(x) + \deg q(x) = \deg(p(x) q(x)) = \deg(1) = 0\text{.}\) Thus \(\deg p(x) = \deg q(x) = 0\text{,}\) and consequently, \(p(x),q(x)\in R^\times\text{.}\)
