Activity 4.3.8.

Answer the questions below. Be prepared to give an information justification of your thinking.
  1. Suppose \(R= \Z\) and \(I = \ideal{8}\text{.}\) Calculate \((3+I) + (7+I)\) and \((4+I)\cdot (6+I)\text{.}\)
  2. Let \(R = \Z_3[x]\) and \(J = \ideal{x^2}\text{.}\) List all elements of \(R/J\) (there should be 9 in total).
  3. Set \(R = \R[x]\) and \(I = \ideal{x^2-1}\text{.}\) Find an element \(f + I \in R/I\) such that \((f+I)(2x+2 + I) = I\text{.}\)
