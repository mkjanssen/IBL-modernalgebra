Activity 3.1.7.

Determine which of the following polynomials are irreducible over the given fields. Justify your answer.

  1. Over \(\Z_2\text{:}\)

    1. \(x^2 + 1\text{,}\)
    2. \(x^2 + x\text{,}\)
    3. \(x^2 +x +1\text{,}\)
    4. \(x^3 + x^2 + 1\text{,}\)
    5. \(x^4 + x^2 + 1\text{.}\)

  2. Over \(\Z_3\text{:}\)

    1. \(x^2 + 1\text{,}\)
    2. \(x^2 + x\text{,}\)
    3. \(x^2 +x +1\text{,}\)
    4. \(x^2 +x +2\text{,}\)
    5. \(x^3 + x +1\text{,}\)
    6. \(x^3 + x^2 + 1\text{,}\)
    7. \(x^3 + x^2 + x + 1\text{.}\)
